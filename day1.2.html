Delicious Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In aother bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingridients into the dry ingridients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a non-stick skilled or griddle over medium heat. Grease lightly with butter or cooking spray.
